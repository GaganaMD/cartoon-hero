Po the Panda

About Po

Po is an inspiration. He is like a mango seed that fell off on a Paddy field. Comparing himself to the others around him was his ultimate go but then being criticized came next naturally. But he decides to stay anyway and spread his roots around and in no time, he shaps into a beautiful mango tree with shooting leaves, blooming flowers, and ripe and fleshy fruits. Maybe that's when the others realise that are better off as a team.
